# Custom Study Schedule: The Triple Crown (PCA + PDE + MLE)
**Launch Date**: March 14, 2026
**Commitment**: 2 hours/day (14 hours/week)
**Base Level**: Intermediate

## Phase 1: Professional Cloud Architect (PCA) - 4 Weeks
*Goal: Solidify infrastructure, hybrid architecture, security, and case studies. This forms the shared foundation for both data and AI workloads.*

- **Week 1 (Mar 14 &ndash; Mar 20): Infrastructure & Networking Basics**
  - Compute (GCE, GKE, App Engine, Cloud Run)
  - Networking (VPC, Peering, Global Load Balancing)
- **Week 2 (Mar 21 &ndash; Mar 27): Security, IAM, and Hybrid Cloud**
  - IAM & Security Controls (VPC-SC, KMS)
  - Interconnect, VPN, Migration Strategies
- **Week 3 (Mar 28 &ndash; Apr 3): Case Study Analysis & Operations**
  - Deep Dive: EHR Healthcare, TerramEarth, Mountkirk Games, Helicopter Racing League
  - Operations Suite (Logging, Monitoring, Trace)
- **Week 4 (Apr 4 &ndash; Apr 10): PCA Exam Simulation & Review**
  - Mock Exams via coach skill
  - Targeted review of weak areas; take the PCA Exam.

## Phase 2: Professional Data Engineer (PDE) - 3 Weeks
*Goal: Master data lifecycle, processing pipelines, and analytics. Built on PCA infrastructure concepts.*

- **Week 5 (Apr 11 &ndash; Apr 17): Big Data Storage & Ingestion**
  - Relational vs NoSQL (Spanner, Bigtable, AlloyDB)
  - BigQuery Deep Dive (Partitioning, Clustering, Administration)
- **Week 6 (Apr 18 &ndash; Apr 24): Data Processing Pipelines**
  - Batch & Streaming (Dataflow, Dataproc, Pub/Sub)
  - Dataform and orchestration basics
- **Week 7 (Apr 25 &ndash; May 1): Data Governance & PDE Exam Simulation**
  - Security, Dataplex, Data Catalog
  - PDE Mock Exams & Final Review; take the PDE Exam.

## Phase 3: Professional Machine Learning Engineer (MLE) - 5 Weeks
*Goal: Master the MLOps lifecycle from framing to production. Built heavily on PDE data foundations (BigQuery) and PCA infrastructure (Vertex AI pipelines).*

- **Week 8 (May 2 &ndash; May 8): ML Problem Framing & Development**
  - Translating business problems to ML; Data prep and feature engineering
  - Frameworks (TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- **Week 9 (May 9 &ndash; May 15): Vertex AI & Model Training**
  - Custom training vs AutoML vs BQML
  - Distributed training and hyperparameter tuning
- **Week 10 (May 16 &ndash; May 22): MLOps & Production**
  - Vertex AI Pipelines, Feature Store, Model Registry
  - CI/CD for ML, Model Monitoring (Data drift)
- **Week 11 (May 23 &ndash; May 29): Generative AI & Foundation Models**
  - Model Garden, Prompt Engineering
  - Vertex AI Agent Builder, implementing RAG architecture
- **Week 12 (May 30 &ndash; June 5): MLE Exam Simulation & Final Review**
  - MLE Mock Exams & Final Review; take the MLE Exam.
